How Much Does a Website Cost in Bangladesh? (2025 Guide)

Published on August 22, 2025 by AJA RETRO

One of the first questions any business owner in Bangladesh asks when considering a new online presence is: "How much will it cost?" The answer isn't always simple, as the price of a website can vary dramatically based on its complexity and features. In this guide, we'll break down the typical costs to give you a clear understanding of what to expect.

A website is an investment, not an expense. A good website will generate more revenue than it costs to build.

Understanding the Key Factors

Before we get to the numbers, let's look at what influences the final price:

  • Number of Pages: A simple 5-page site is cheaper than a 20-page corporate site.
  • Custom Design vs. Template: A unique, custom-designed website will cost more than one built from a pre-made template.
  • E-commerce Functionality: Selling products online requires complex features like payment gateways, which increases the cost.
  • Content Management System (CMS): A site with a CMS (like WordPress) that allows you to update content yourself costs more upfront but saves money later.

Typical Website Price Ranges in Bangladesh

Here are some realistic price ranges you can expect in the current market.

1. Basic Static Website (10,000tk - 25,000tk)

This is the entry-level option, perfect for small businesses, portfolios, or individuals who need a professional online brochure. It's a "what you see is what you get" site where the developer makes any updates.

  • Pages: 3-5 pages (e.g., Home, About, Services, Contact).
  • Features: Mobile-responsive design, contact form, social media links.
  • Best for: Businesses that don't need to update content frequently.

2. Standard Business Website with CMS (25,000tk - 60,000tk)

This is the most popular choice for growing businesses. Built on a platform like WordPress, it gives you the power to manage your own content, such as adding blog posts or updating service information.

  • Pages: 5-15 pages.
  • Features: Everything in the basic package, plus a blog, admin dashboard, and basic SEO setup.
  • Best for: Companies that want to actively manage their online presence and content marketing.

3. E-commerce Website (50,000tk - 200,000tk+)

For businesses looking to sell products online, an e-commerce site is essential. The **e-commerce website price in Bangladesh** varies widely based on complexity.

  • Features: Product pages, shopping cart, secure checkout, integration with local payment gateways (bKash, Nagad, etc.), and an order management system.
  • The price depends heavily on: The number of products, complexity of shipping rules, and any custom features required.

Don't Forget Ongoing Costs

Building the site is just the first step. Remember to budget for these recurring costs:

  • Domain Name: Around 1,500tk per year for a .com domain.
