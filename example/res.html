Our Story

Born from a passion for authentic Bangladeshi cuisine, Kachchi House was founded to bring the true taste of royal biryani to the heart of the city. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, perfected over time to deliver an unforgettable culinary experience.

We use only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients, from fragrant chinigura rice to tender, slow-cooked mutton, ensuring every dish is a celebration of flavor and tradition.
